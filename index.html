About Us

At INPROSUM, we are committed to providing outstanding industrial solutions based on quality and performance to meet the diverse needs of our global customers in the oil, infrastructure, IT and general industrial sectors.

Our core business is the supply of world-class products and services, supported by a team of highly experienced professionals in equipment sales, consulting and engineering project development. With a comprehensive approach, we facilitate the import and export of products and provide end-to-end solutions tailored to the needs of the industry.

Vision

To establish INPROSUM as a global leader with a competitive edge in the distribution of premier brands and industrial equipment in the oil, infrastructure, IT and general industrial markets. To be distinguished by our commitment to innovation, operational excellence and impactful contributions in the international arena.
